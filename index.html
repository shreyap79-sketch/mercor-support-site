FAQs

Form I-983 signing

Q: My university told me I need you to sign my Form I-983. Who should I send this to to get it signed?

A: Thanks for checking. Mercor isn’t able to sign I-983 forms because we don’t hire contractors under the STEM OPT program. Your best next step is to contact your university’s international student office—they’ll point you to the right authorized signer.

First payout timing

Q: I’m in my second week and it’s Thursday. You said I’d be paid each Wednesday for the week prior—why don’t I see any money yet?

A: Totally get that the first payment feels time-sensitive. We initiate payouts on Wednesdays. For the very first payout, Stripe applies a one-time 7-day hold while verifying your account, and some banks take an extra 3–5 business days to show the funds. The upside: once this first one clears, future payouts generally land by Thursday after we initiate on Wednesday.

  • Payments are already moving through Stripe once you see the “initiated” status.
  • If something seems off after the expected window, reach out and we’ll check it.

Resume upload 404

Q: When I go to https://work.mercor.com/upload-resume I get a 404. How do I upload?

A: That old link isn’t active. Instead: log in at work.mercor.com/home → open Profile from the left menu → use the first tab (Resume) to upload or update your file. The moment it’s uploaded, it’s visible to clients. If you see file-type or size errors, ping us and we’ll help.

Referral bonus eligibility

Q: I’ve referred 40 people to Mercor roles but haven’t been paid. When will I get the bonus?

A: We love strong referral activity. The $500 bonus triggers once a referral is hired and completes at least 10 hours of work. Applications alone don’t qualify. When one of your referrals is placed and hits 10 hours, the bonus is automatically added to your account.

Referrals Dashboard

The Referrals Dashboard gives you a live view of who you’ve invited, where they are in the process, and when you get paid.

Getting started

  1. Log in to your Mercor account and open Referrals from the left menu.
  2. At the top you’ll see: Total Referrals, Total Earning Potential, Total Earned, and Next Payment.
  3. Use Invite Connections to import LinkedIn contacts or upload a file for bulk outreach.

Making a referral

Activate your referral link on any job listing, then share your unique link. Tracking is automatic.

When you get paid

  • $500 per referral after they accept an offer and complete 10 hours.
  • Paid weekly via Stripe or Wise, aligned with contractor payouts.

Status tracking

Real-time visibility into offer acceptance, hours worked, and whether your bonus is scheduled or paid.

SQL Examples

Users who applied to Mercor listings

SELECT DISTINCT
  u.UserId,
  u.Email
FROM Applications a
JOIN Listings    l ON l.ListingUID = a.ListingVersionId
JOIN Company     c ON c.CompanyId  = l.CompanyId
JOIN MercorUsers u ON u.UserId     = a.UserId
WHERE c.Name = 'Mercor';

Average min/max listing rates (USD)

SELECT
  AVG(l.RateMin) / 100.0 AS AvgMinRateUSD,
  AVG(l.RateMax) / 100.0 AS AvgMaxRateUSD
FROM Listings l
JOIN Company  c ON c.CompanyId = l.CompanyId
WHERE c.Name = 'Mercor';
