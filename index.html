Question 1:
My university told me I need you to sign my form I-983. Who should I send this to to get it signed?

Answer 1

Thank you for reaching out. Mercor does not engage contractors under STEM OPT status and does not sign immigration or university forms such as the I-983. For next steps, the right contact is the employer of record (the entity that directly employs and supervises you). If the employer of record is unclear, review the Contracts tab in the Mercor dashboard or your onboarding emails. University international student services can also advise on who must sign. For general assistance, contact support@mercor.com.

Q2: When you update your work experiences on the Resume page, what internal endpoint is called and with which HTTP Method? Is this the right HTTP method to use, why or why not?

Answer

Endpoint & Method

PATCH /api/resume/experiences/{experienceId}

Why PATCH is correct

  • PATCH is intended for partial updates (modify only selected fields like company, role, description, startDate, endDate).
  • PUT implies a full replacement of the resource, risking unintended field overwrites if not all values are provided.
  • POST is for creating a new resource, not updating an existing one.

Conclusion: PATCH is the REST-compliant choice for editing individual work experiences.

Q1: Users who applied to listings created by ‚ÄúMercor‚Äù

SELECT DISTINCT
  u.UserId,
  u.Email
FROM Applications a
JOIN Listings    l ON l.ListingUID = a.ListingVersionId
JOIN Company     c ON c.CompanyId  = l.CompanyId
JOIN MercorUsers u ON u.UserId     = a.UserId
WHERE c.Name = 'Mercor';

Q2: Average min/max rate (USD) for listings created by ‚ÄúMercor‚Äù

SELECT
  AVG(l.RateMin) / 100.0 AS AvgMinRateUSD,
  AVG(l.RateMax) / 100.0 AS AvgMaxRateUSD
FROM Listings l
JOIN Company  c ON c.CompanyId = l.CompanyId
WHERE c.Name = 'Mercor';

Note: rates are stored in cents; divide by 100 to convert to USD.
