FAQs

Form I-983 signing

Q: My university told me I need you to sign my Form I-983. Who should I send this to to get it signed?

A: Mercor isn’t able to sign I-983 forms because we don’t hire contractors under the STEM OPT program. Contact your university’s international student office for the appropriate signer.

First payout timing

Q: I’m in my second week and it’s Thursday. You said I’d be paid each Wednesday for the week prior—why don’t I see any money yet?

A: Payouts are initiated on Wednesdays. For the first payment, Stripe applies a 7-day hold while verifying your account, and some banks take an extra 3–5 business days to clear. Once this initial payout is complete, future payments usually land by Thursday.

Resume upload 404

Q: When I go to https://work.mercor.com/upload-resume I get a 404. How do I upload?

A: Use your dashboard instead: log in at work.mercor.com/home → open Profile → first tab (Resume) to upload or update your file.

Referral bonus eligibility

Q: I’ve referred 40 people to Mercor roles but haven’t been paid. When will I get the bonus?

A: The $500 bonus is triggered once a referral is hired and completes at least 10 hours of work. Applications alone do not qualify.

SQL Examples

Users who applied to Mercor listings

SELECT DISTINCT
  u.UserId,
  u.Email
FROM Applications a
JOIN Listings    l ON l.ListingUID = a.ListingVersionId
JOIN Company     c ON c.CompanyId  = l.CompanyId
JOIN MercorUsers u ON u.UserId     = a.UserId
WHERE c.Name = 'Mercor';

Average min/max listing rates (USD)

SELECT
  AVG(l.RateMin) / 100.0 AS AvgMinRateUSD,
  AVG(l.RateMax) / 100.0 AS AvgMaxRateUSD
FROM Listings l
JOIN Company  c ON c.CompanyId = l.CompanyId
WHERE c.Name = 'Mercor';
