Roleplay — User Questions & Answers

Q1. My university told me I need you to sign my form I-983. Who should I send this to?

Answer: Mercor does not sign I-983 forms. The I-983 must be signed by your employer of record (the company that directly employs/supervises you). If you’re unsure who that is, check your Contracts tab or onboarding email. Need help? Email support@mercor.com.

Q2. It’s Thursday in my second week. You said I’d be paid Wednesday for last week. Why is there no money?

  • Payouts process on Wednesdays for approved hours from the prior week.
  • Banks can take 1–3 business days to settle. It’s normal not to see funds until Thu/Fri.
  • If there’s still nothing by Friday EOD, confirm your payout details are complete and contact support.

Q3. I get a 404 at https://work.mercor.com/upload-resume. How do I get past this?

Answer: That path is outdated. Upload your resume from the Applications tab after you sign in. If it still fails, clear cache or try another browser; if blocked, contact support for a fresh link.

Q4. I’ve referred 40 people but haven’t been paid. When will I get paid?

  • Referral rewards pay only when a referred candidate is hired and meets eligibility (e.g., minimum time worked).
  • Track each referral’s status and payout state in your Referrals dashboard.

Documentation — Referrals Dashboard: How to Use

  1. Open Dashboard: Sign in → left nav → Referrals.
  2. Share Your Link: Copy your unique referral link and share it with candidates.
  3. Status Meanings:
    • Invited — person clicked your link
    • Applied — submitted an application
    • In Process — under review/interviews
    • Hired — offer accepted
    • Completed — eligibility met; payout queued
  4. Payout Tracking: Once a referral reaches Completed, the reward appears in the payout queue. Ensure your payout method is set up in your profile.
  5. Troubleshooting: If a referral didn’t attach to you, ask the candidate to reapply using your link, or contact support with their email so we can attribute it.

Application Debugging (Chrome DevTools)

Find interviewId & status for “Talent Success Interview”
  1. Open Interviews → F12 → Network → filter interview.
  2. Click the card to trigger a request → open it → Response → read id/status.
Resume → Work Experience: endpoint & method
  1. Edit an item & Save with Network open.
  2. Expected: PATCH (partial update). PUT is OK if replacing whole object. POST should be for create/upsert.
Find listing created time
  1. Filter Network for listing and read createdAt in response, or search __NEXT_DATA__ for it.
  2. Convert in Console: new Date("2025-09-04T12:34:56.789Z").toString()

SQL Queries

Users who applied to listings created by “Mercor”

SELECT DISTINCT
  u.UserId,
  u.Email
FROM Applications a
JOIN Listings    l ON l.ListingUID = a.ListingVersionId
JOIN Company     c ON c.CompanyId  = l.CompanyId
JOIN MercorUsers u ON u.UserId     = a.UserId
WHERE c.Name = 'Mercor';

Average min/max rate (USD) for “Mercor” listings

SELECT
  AVG(l.RateMin) / 100.0 AS AvgMinRateUSD,
  AVG(l.RateMax) / 100.0 AS AvgMaxRateUSD
FROM Listings l
JOIN Company  c ON c.CompanyId = l.CompanyId
WHERE c.Name = 'Mercor';

Rates are stored in cents → divide by 100.
